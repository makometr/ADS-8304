Shannon-Fano coding algorithm

  1. The input comes in order of non-increasing frequency data.
  2. There is a middle that divides the alphabet into approximately two parts. These parts (sums of alphabet frequencies) are approximately equal.
  3. For the left side, “0” is assigned, for the right side “1”, so we get the leaves of the tree.
  4. Step 2 is repeated until we get a single element of the sequence, i.e. sheet
